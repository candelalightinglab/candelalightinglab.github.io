A night landscape composed entirely in light. A stone gateway frames a causeway over a pool set with points of light like stars, and floodlit basalt cliffs are mirrored in the still water.

Uplit trees and grazed stone surfaces build depth through the garden after dark, turning the site into a sequence of lit thresholds.
