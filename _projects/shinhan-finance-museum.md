A permanent exhibition on the history of Korean finance, staged in a sequence of dark galleries. Backlit document cases, a warmly lit hanok architectural model and an immersive projection room give the material a quiet, archival gravity.

Accent light is kept tight on the artefacts while circulation is held low, letting the displays lead the way through the story.
