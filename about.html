About

캔델라 건축조명연구소는 건축·박물관·전시 공간의 빛을 설계합니다. 유물과 공간이 스스로 말하도록, 빛은 절제됩니다.

Candela Lighting Lab designs lighting for architecture, museums and exhibitions — led by Seungjoo Baik, Architectural Lighting Design Consultant. Seungjoo trained at Parsons School of Design (MFA in Lighting) and practiced in New York with Cline Bettridge Bernstein Lighting Design before founding the studio in Seoul.
